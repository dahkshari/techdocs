# Important AI Concepts 

Most generative AI agents rely on frameworks like Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) and the concept of “human in the loop” (HITL) to function optimally. Technical writers are critical for both.

## Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) 

RAG enhances large language models (LLMs) by enabling them to access external knowledge bases for real-time information. This process minimizes hallucinations in AI responses and enables complex problem-solving capabilities.

RAG agents are most effective when they’re pointed to highly organized, interconnected data sources and knowledge bases. 

Sources that are clearly written and logically structured. 

This is why the success of RAG AI agents starts with documentation writers.

## Human In The Loop (HITL) 

HITL emphasizes human involvement to ensure the accuracy and relevance of generative AI responses. Technical writers, with their understanding of human audiences, can guide AI, address biases and ambiguities, fact-check, and facilitate continuous improvement.
